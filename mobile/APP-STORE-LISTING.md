## Version Information

### Description

NagLead is the lead tracker that won't shut up until you call your leads back.

Built for solo plumbers, electricians, cleaners, handymen, and other service pros who lose jobs because they forget to follow up. Not because they lack skills. Because the phone rang while they were under a sink, and by the time they checked, the lead had already called someone else.

HOW IT WORKS:
- Add a lead in 5 seconds: name + what they need. That's it.
- Get escalating push notifications: 2 hours, 6 hours, 24 hours, 48 hours. Each one louder than the last.
- Call or text directly from the lead card with one tap.
- Mark leads as replied, won, or lost to track your close rate.

KEY FEATURES:
- Escalating nag reminders that get more aggressive over time
- Call Back and Snooze buttons directly on notifications
- Auto-add leads from email (forward Yelp, Thumbtack, Angi, and website form emails)
- Monthly scorecard showing wins, losses, and revenue
- Snooze leads when you can't respond right now
- Quiet hours so you're not nagged at midnight
- Free for up to 5 active leads, forever

NagLead is not a CRM. It does one thing: makes sure you never forget to call someone back. One returned call that converts to a $200 job pays for an entire year.

## App Review Information

### Review Notes

NagLead is a lead reminder app for solo service businesses (plumbers, cleaners, electricians, handymen). The core feature is escalating push notifications that remind users to call their leads back.

Push notifications are essential to the app's function. The entire value proposition is that the app nags you until you respond to your leads. Without notifications, the app cannot fulfill its purpose.

The app requires account creation because leads are synced across devices via our backend (Supabase). There is also a web version at https://naglead.com/app.
